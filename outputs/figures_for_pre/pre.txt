Our explanation: Zero123++ relies on view-dependent texture cues — highlights, reflections — to recover 3D structure. When those cues are absent, the model falls back on its generative prior, hallucinating confidently."

### SLIDE 7 — Confound analysis (30s)

**标题**：A subtle finding — material × angle interaction

**视觉**：一个简单 2×3 表（直接在 slide 上打字）：

**Bullet points**（表格旁边）：

- **But** Lambertian surfaces are disproportionately hurt (3.3× drop)
- → Texture cues partially compensate for difficult viewpoints

**演讲稿**：
